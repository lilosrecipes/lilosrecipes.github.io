(SERVES 6-8)

## Ingredients
* 3 large jars tomato sauce
* 2 eggs
* 2 cups ricotta cheese
* 12 lasagna noodles
* 1/3 Spinach chopped
* nutmeg
* 1 lb Mozzarella
* 1/2 Parmesan or Ramano grated
* Kosher salt

## Directions
### Filling:
1. Beat 2 eggs
2. Add Ricotta, Nutmeg, Salt, Pepper, Spinach
### In pan layer the following:
1. Sauce
2. 1/3 noodles
3. ½ ricotta mixture
4. Repeat 2 more times
5. Cover with Parm/Romano
6. Cook at 375°
