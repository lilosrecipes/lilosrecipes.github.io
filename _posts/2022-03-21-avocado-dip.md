## Ingredients
* 2 Avocados mashed
* 1 Hard-Boiled Egg
* 2 tablespoons Mayonnaise
* 1 tsp Garlic Powder
* 1/4 tsp Ketchup

# Directions
1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Leave in refrigerator for at least 2 hours
3. Serve with crackers
